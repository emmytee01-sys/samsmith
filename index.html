Welcome to Sam Smith Experience

Located on the stunning coast of Golden Bay, Sam Smith is designed to integrate seamlessly with the natural landscape. Built high upon the stone cliffs, our suites offer panoramic views of the water, custom architectural highlights, and absolute privacy.

Each of our rooms incorporates state-of-the-art climate control, automated smart amenities, custom bedding designed by Rasmus Andersson, and organic Mediterranean spa toiletries. Our services include:

  • 24/7 dedicated private butler service for suites
  • Michelin-starred restaurant dining and oceanfront wine bars
  • Infinity cliffside swimming pools and direct private beach access
  • Daily luxury yacht excursions and sightseeing charters

We are dedicated to providing an unforgettable, premium experience that redefines coastal luxury. We look forward to hosting your dream retreat.
